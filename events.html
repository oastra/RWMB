Upcoming Events

Gear Up: Upcoming Events with the Rock Wallaby Mountain Bike Club

Peak Performance: High Altitude Challenge

Challenge yourself with the "Peak Performance: High Altitude Challenge" in the majestic Blue Mountains. Scheduled for next month, this high-altitude event invites seasoned riders to tackle steep climbs and rugged terrains, offering breathtaking views from the top. It’s an exhilarating adventure that tests your endurance and rewards you with incredible panoramas.

Blue Mountains, New South Wales
Riverside Rally: Family Fun Ride

Gather your family and head to the "Riverside Rally: Family Fun Ride" at South Bank Parklands this Saturday. Perfect for riders of all ages and skill levels, this event offers a relaxed ride along the Brisbane River. Enjoy the lush scenery and vibrant atmosphere of one of Queensland’s most beloved parks, complete with activities and attractions for the whole family.

South Bank Parklands, Queensland
Forest Frenzy: Cross-Country Race

Prepare for the thrill of the "Forest Frenzy: Cross-Country Race" in Kalamunda. This exciting event, taking place next weekend, features a challenging course through Western Australia’s famed mountain biking trails. Ideal for competitive riders, this race combines speed, skill, and strategy in a beautiful forest setting.

Kalamunda, Western Australia
Sunset Spokes: Evening Ride Series

Cap off your day with our "Sunset Spokes: Evening Ride Series" at the scenic Lakeview Trails. Every Thursday evening, join fellow club members for a serene ride under the setting sun. This series is perfect for those who enjoy a peaceful ride with stunning views. Unwind with us while we cycle through some of Springfield's most beautiful landscapes at dusk.

Lysterfield Park, Victoria
